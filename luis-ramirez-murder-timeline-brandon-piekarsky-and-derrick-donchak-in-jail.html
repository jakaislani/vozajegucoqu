    Luis Ramirez Murder Timeline: What Happened? His Death Explained

    Luis Ramirez was beaten to death in July 2008 just because of his racial profile. Four local footballers beat him to death while shouting racial slurs and threats.

    After two days of the incident, Ramirez died from the injury to the head from the beating. The news of his death took over media in an instant as Latino communities all over the country condemned the unfortunate incident.

    The anti-Latino hate crime was alarming at that time and the Hispanic community came together against the racial hate crime.
