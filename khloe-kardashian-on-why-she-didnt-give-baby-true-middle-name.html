    Like her sisters Kim Kardashian and Kylie Jenner, Khloé Kardashian chose not to give her baby True Thompson a middle name. But there was a reasonable explanation behind this choice, Khloé explained in a new blog post on her site. Also, it could change: Khloé isn't firmly committed to depriving True of a middle name for life.

    "True doesn't have a middle name—for now," she wrote. "It was enough pressure to pick a first name! I have a couple of names in mind, I'm just not completely sure about it yet. I plan to take my time and decide if I'll give her one at all. I can add it at any time and it's easy to get on the birth certificate."

    Kim's kids Saint, North, and Chicago West don't have middle names; Kylie's daughter Stormi Webster doesn't either.
