    M&M’s loves to mix it up with their flavors, especially when a holiday rolls around. This year, they’re debuting White Chocolate Strawberry Shake just in time for Valentine’s Day. Find out if we absolutely fell in love with this flavor—or if we think it should get dumped.

    New Product!

    White Chocolate Strawberry Shake M&M’sTargetWalmart

    As an M&M’s lover, I’m super disappointed with these White Chocolate Strawberry Shake M&M’s. They do not taste good! The strawberry flavor is very artificial and the coating is thick. These don’t remind me of a strawberry shake—it’s more like strawberry milk if you added way, way too much syrup.

    Honestly, the best thing about these M&M’s is the fact that the Green M&M is on the packaging. I’m just now realizing she doesn’t have a name so I’m capitalizing the “g” in green because I guess her name is…Green? She’s hot and I like seeing her. Just marvel at her beauty on the bag instead of buying and eating these M&M’s.

    Rating:

    2/10
