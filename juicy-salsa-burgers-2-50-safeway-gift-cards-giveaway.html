    Juicy Salsa Burgers

    Thanks to Hellman’s, Safeway, and Mario Batali for sponsoring this post! Juicy Salsa Burgers are a great way to serve up a twist on a classic!

    Course Dinner Cuisine American Keyword juicy salsa burgers Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Cook Time 15 minutes minutes Total Time 20 minutes minutes Servings 8 Calories 338kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 2 lbs ground chuck
    • 1/2 onion peeled and grated
    • 3 tbsp Best Foods Mayonnaise
    • 3 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
    • 1 tbsp chili powder optional
    • 1/2 cup salsa fire roasted preferred
    • salt and pepper to taste

    Instructions

    • Preheat grill.

    • Mix ground chuck, mayonnaise, onion, Worcestershire sauce, and chili powder together.

    • Season with salt and pepper.

    • Form patties gently.

    • Grill to desired temperature.

    • Top burgers with salsa and serve.
