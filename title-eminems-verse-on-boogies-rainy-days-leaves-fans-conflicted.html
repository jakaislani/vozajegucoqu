    Eminem's Verse On Boogie's &quot;Rainy Days&quot; Leaves Fans Conflicted

    Twitter wasnt quite sure what to make of Eminems verse on Boogies Rainy Days cut. Taken from the Shady Records signees debut album,Everythings For Sale, the track contains a questionable line from Em that has something to do with sheep and sexual intercourse.

    13 May 2024, 00:00

    Twitter wasn’t quite sure what to make of Eminem’s verse on Boogie’s “Rainy Days” cut. Taken from the Shady Records signee’s debut album,Everything’s For Sale, the track contains a questionable line from Em that has something to do with sheep and sexual intercourse.

    “I left my legacy hurt? Fuckin’ absurd/Like a shepherd havin’ sex with his sheep, fuck what you heard/All this talk in my ear, I got a idea/Like the clerk when you tryna buy beer (ID ya),” he spits.

    Fans flooded the social media platform to argue about Shady’s contribution. Some thought the entire verse was brilliant, while others believed it simply destroyed the song. There were also a few people just wondering what the hell that sheep line was all about.

    AD LOADING...

    Of course, Em has fired back at his detractors before. His pent up anger was essentially the crux of Kamikaze, so it won’t be surprising if Shady has something to say to those criticizing his work on this track.
