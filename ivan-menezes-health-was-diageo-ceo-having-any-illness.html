    Ivan Menezes Biography

    Born on 10th July 1959 to Manuel Menezes, the chairman of the Indian Railway Board, Ivan Menezes grew up in an environment that instilled in him a deep appreciation for hard work and ambition. He embarked on his educational journey at St. Stephen’s College in Delhi, where he honed his intellectual abilities and developed a passion for business. Further pursuing his academic pursuits, Menezes earned a degree from the prestigious Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad, where he gained a solid foundation in management principles.

    Recognizing the importance of continuous learning, Menezes went on to attend Northwestern University’s Kellogg School of Management, where he further honed his leadership skills and embraced a global perspective on business. Armed with a formidable educational background, he joined Diageo in 1997, beginning a journey that would see him rise through the ranks and become an influential figure within the company.

    Throughout his tenure at Diageo, Menezes held several senior positions that showcased his versatility and adaptability. He served as the chief operating officer, chairman of Latin America & the Caribbean, chairman of Asia Pacific, president and CEO of Diageo North America, and chief operating officer of Diageo North America. Each role allowed him to garner invaluable experience and develop a deep understanding of the nuances of the global market.

    Beyond his contributions to Diageo, Menezes also made his mark on the broader business landscape. As a non-executive director of Coach, Inc., a prominent fashion retailer based in the United States, he brought his expertise and acumen to the table, contributing to the growth and success of the company.

    One of the defining moments of Menezes’ career came in 2013 when he assumed the role of CEO of Diageo, succeeding Paul S. Walsh. Under his leadership, Diageo witnessed remarkable growth and strategic transformation. Menezes’ keen business sense and unwavering commitment to excellence propelled the company to new heights, solidifying its position as a leader in the alcoholic beverages industry.

    Beyond his professional accomplishments, Ivan Menezes was a champion for equality and social progress. His unwavering commitment to promoting diversity and inclusion within the corporate sphere earned him well-deserved recognition. In the 2023 New Year Honours, Menezes was knighted for his exceptional services to business and equality, cementing his status as a trailblazer in the fight for a more inclusive and equitable society.
